Privacy Policy

Newsly · Last updated: 14 April 2026

This policy describes how Newsly (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application (“App”). Review it alongside Apple’s App Privacy labels in App Store Connect and update both if your practices change.

What the App does

Newsly shows news articles from third-party sources, personalises your feed based on how you use the App, offers optional audio and AI-assisted features, and may offer subscriptions. We do not operate the news websites you open from the App; their own terms and policies apply when you visit them.

Information collected and why

  • Usage and preferences on your device. For example: articles you view, skip, bookmark, or play; category preferences; and settings. This helps rank and personalise content and is stored primarily on your device (for example via local storage).
  • Voice audio (optional). If you use voice search, audio may be sent to a speech-recognition service to produce text. We do not use it for advertising.
  • Text you send to AI features (optional). If you use explanations, quizzes, or similar tools, article-related text may be sent to an AI provider to generate a response.
  • Purchases and subscriptions. Payments are processed by Apple (and on Android, Google). We use a subscription management service to verify your plan and entitlements — not your full payment card number.
  • Diagnostics (if enabled). The App or third-party SDKs may collect limited technical data (for example crash or performance data) to keep the App reliable. Adjust this where your device or the App offers controls.

Third-party services

The App relies on services such as: news/content APIs; cloud AI providers for optional features; speech services for voice input; subscription/payment verification; and, where applicable, analytics or attribution. Each has its own privacy policy. We choose providers to process data only as needed to provide the App.

Retention

On-device data remains until you delete the App or clear app data where your OS allows. Data held by service providers is retained according to their policies and our agreements, typically for a limited time for security and operations.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). Do not use the App if you are under that age.

Your choices and rights

You can control permissions (such as microphone) in your device settings. You may contact us to ask questions, or to exercise privacy rights available in your region (for example access or deletion), subject to applicable law. We will verify reasonable requests.

Changes
