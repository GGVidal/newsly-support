How to use the app

Basics

  • Home — Your main feed. The more you read, skip, or bookmark, the better Newsly can match stories to your interests.
  • Discover — Browse news by category when you want to explore beyond your feed.
  • Bookmarks — Tap the bookmark icon on an article to save it. Open the Bookmarks tab to see everything you saved.
  • Article — Open any story for the full view. Use listen / play where available to hear the article read aloud.
  • Voice search — Where the app offers it, use voice to search the news instead of typing (microphone permission may be required).
  • Profile — Streaks, stats, and app settings.

Some features (for example extra AI tools or daily limits) may depend on your plan — check in the app for what is included.
